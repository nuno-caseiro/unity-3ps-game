Race track around a lake. 

This asset contains:

- race track model
- a bridge
- Unity terrain
- water static texture
- safety tires and road signs
- one camera
- two lights (sun+ambient)
- rocks
- electrical towers

Materials with medium-res textures (1024x1024px). 

Models are low-poly.
